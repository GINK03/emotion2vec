request-host does not domain-match the Domain\n     attribute.\n\n   * The request-host is a FQDN (not IP address) and has the form HD,\n     where D is the value of the Domain attribute, and H is a string\n     that contains one or more dots.\n\n   Examples:\n\n   * A Set-Cookie from request-host y.x.foo.com for Domain=.foo.com\n     would be rejected, because H is y.x and contains a
